# Code Of Conduct

AI Project Starter is a public documentation and skill package. Keep discussion professional, specific, and safe to publish.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Keep examples fictional unless the data is intentionally public.
- Report security, credential, private-prompt, or disclosure issues privately through the security policy.
- Do not post secrets, tokens, private URLs, customer data, local logs, screenshots, or internal company material in issues or pull requests.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data or credentials.
- Repeated off-topic comments that prevent maintainers from triaging the work.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these rules.
